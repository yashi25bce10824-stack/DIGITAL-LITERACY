📌 Project Overview

This project is developed as part of my role as a Student Digital Ambassador. The objective is to promote digital literacy among students and help them understand how to use digital tools responsibly, professionally, and effectively.

The portfolio covers various aspects of digital literacy, including creating awareness content, building a professional online presence, practising coding and collaboration tools, understanding email etiquette, and learning about cybercrime and online safety.

⸻

🎯 Project Objectives
	•	To understand the concept of digital literacy beyond basic usage
	•	To build a professional digital identity
	•	To practise using important academic and technical platforms
	•	To promote safe and responsible online behavior
	•	To improve communication skills in digital environments
