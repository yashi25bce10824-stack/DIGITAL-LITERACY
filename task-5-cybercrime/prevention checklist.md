# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## ✅ Tips for College Students

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.  
- Always verify payment requests before approving them on UPI apps.  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes, as they may lead to money being deducted instead of received.  
- Use strong, unique passwords for all your accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media.  
- Keep your apps and devices updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.  
- Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on unsecured networks.  
- Think before sharing personal information or photos online to protect your digital identity.  

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime

- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
