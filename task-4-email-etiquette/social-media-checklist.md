# Social Media Checklist for Responsible Use

## ✅ Do's
- Think before posting or sharing content  
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication  
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation  
- Maintain a respectful and polite tone in comments and messages  
- Adjust privacy settings to control who can view your content  

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or negative discussions  
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown sources  
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content  
- Do not overshare details about your personal life
